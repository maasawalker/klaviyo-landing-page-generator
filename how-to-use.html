How to create your custom landing page

1

Open the generator and load your JD

Open the generator URL. In the Smart JD Importer, paste your job description URL and click Fetch — or paste the JD text directly into the text box.

If Fetch doesn't work, open the JD in your browser, press Cmd+A (select all), Cmd+C (copy), and paste the text into the box.
2

Add candidate persona context (optional but recommended)

Click a Quick preset button that matches your role type — Engineering, Marketing, Sales, Product, or People. This tells the AI what motivates your target candidate, which makes the Why Klaviyo section and CTA significantly more compelling.

Have custom EVP notes or persona research? Upload a .txt or .docx file instead, or paste it directly.
3

Click "Parse & autofill form"

The AI reads your JD and fills in every section of the form — hero headline, subtitle, role details, qualifications, Why Klaviyo cards, culture cards, ticker phrases, and CTA. Fields turn yellow when autofilled. A completeness score shows what's still missing.

4

Review and edit

Review yellow-highlighted fields — the AI is good but your judgment is better. Key things to check: hero headline tone, qualifications (should match JD exactly), and Why Klaviyo cards (should speak to this specific candidate type). The live preview on the right updates instantly.

Toggle between Desktop and Mobile preview using the buttons above the preview panel to check how it looks on phone.
5

Add a quote (optional)

Scroll to the Quotes section. Add a testimonial if you have one handy — if not, no sweat! Click Find testimonial candidates on LinkedIn — a pre-filtered search opens showing current Klaviyo employees in the relevant department. Reach out using the Copy outreach DM template. Once you have a quote, upload their photo and paste the text.

6

Copy the HTML and deploy

Click Copy HTML in the top right. Then follow the deploy steps below to get your page live with a shareable URL.

Tips for best results

  • Always use a persona preset — it dramatically improves the Why Klaviyo and CTA copy
  • Paste the full JD text (including qualifications) for the most complete parse
  • Review the hero headline first — it's the most important field and may need a human touch
  • Add a real employee quote — it's the #1 trust signal for candidates
  • Fill in your live URL in the Analytics & Sharing section to get UTM share links

What the AI fills automatically

  • Hero headline + subtitle (candidate-facing, action-oriented)
  • Salary pills and role descriptor pills
  • What you're building + how you'll spend your time
  • All required and preferred qualifications (verbatim from JD)
  • Tech skills and soft skills tags
  • Why Klaviyo section (4 cards, tailored to candidate type)
  • Ticker phrases, CTA title and subtitle

How to deploy your page live

01

Go to github.com → create a free account if you don't have one → create a new public repository (name it anything, e.g. senior-ai-engineer-page)

02

In the new repo, click "Add file → Create new file". Name it index.html. Paste the copied HTML. Click Commit changes.

03

Go to vercel.com → create a free account → click "Add New Project" → import your GitHub repo → click Deploy. Done. You have a live URL in ~30 seconds.

04

To update the page: go back to the generator, make your edits, copy the new HTML, go to your GitHub repo → click index.html → pencil icon → paste → commit. Vercel auto-redeploys in ~30 seconds.
